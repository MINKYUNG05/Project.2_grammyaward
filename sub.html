      Wesley Bonner - Senior Vice President, Marketing & Audience Development at BDG

      Webby Award winner BDG is one of today's leading global media companies, and as their Senior Vice President of Marketing & Audience Development, Wesley Bonner is leading the charge when it comes to crafting successful marketing campaigns. Bonner is also a new executive judge for The Webbys. We sat down with him to discuss upcoming internet trends and what work he is excited to see at this year's Webby Awards.

      Tim Nolan - Creative Partner at PRODUCT™

      Tim Nolan is a veteran in the creative industry, and is a partner for new creative agency PRODUCT. He is also a longtime Executive Webby Judge, where he has reviewed the industry's best work for over a decade, and even helped us celebrate the 25th anniversary of the Web. Over the next few months, he's lending his expertise to help select nominees for the 27th Annual Webby Awards. We caught up with Tim about what he brings to the Academy, and his latest endeavor.
