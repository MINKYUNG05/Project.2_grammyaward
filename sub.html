    • Judges Spotlight


      Wesley Bonner - Senior Vice President, Marketing & Audience Development at BDG

      Grammy Award winner BDG is one of today's leading global media companies, and as their Senior Vice President of Marketing & Audience Development, Wesley Bonner is leading the charge when it comes to crafting successful marketing campaigns. Bonner is also a new executive judge for The Grammys. We sat down with him to discuss upcoming internet trends and what work he is excited to see at this year's Grammy Awards.


      November 8,2022

    • Judges Spotlight


      Tim Nolan - Creative Partner at PRODUCT™

      Tim Nolan is a veteran in the creative industry, and is a partner for new creative agency PRODUCT. He is also a longtime Executive Grammy Judge, where he has reviewed the industry's best work for over a decade, and even helped us celebrate the 25th anniversary of the Web. Over the next few months, he's lending his expertise to help select nominees for the 27th Annual Grammy Awards. We caught up with Tim about what he brings to the Academy, and his latest endeavor.


      December 17,2022

    • Judges Spotlight


      Zahra Jabini – Senior Vice President Of Engineering, BDG

      As the Senior Vice President Of Engineering at BDG (formerly Bustle Digital Group), Zahra Jabini brings her expertise to a broad range of responsibilities from supporting brand relaunches to managing the entire engineering team. Zahra is also a newly appointed Grammy judge, and will be reviewing this year's Grammy entries! We sat down together to discuss her thoughts on the future of tech and what she is excited to see this season.


      December 1, 2022
